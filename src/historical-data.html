How much did Brussels invest
in bicycle paths?

The total amount of cyclable paths is calculated in kilometers and includes the ones separated from the main roads as well as the suggested paths or the Regional Cyclable Itineraries (RCI). The RCIs are suggested paths for cyclists willing to commute on a medium to long distance. These itineraries take into account the calmest streets and the less stressful paths for the users. The accuracy of the really past data (1896-1970) are less reliable in terms of consistency. Nonetheless, the overall data remain as close as possible to the reality and can be trustworthy on a holistic vision of the infrastructure evolution.
