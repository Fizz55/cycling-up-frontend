How many cyclists circulate on the Brussels infrastructure?

The evolution of the number of cyclists shows a striking rise between 1998 and 2017. This increase is supported by the continuous evolution of the bicycle infrastructure in the region of Brussels.

The evolution of the number of cyclists variates according to the seasons. These ones are defined by the following months: January, May, September and November. This graph depicts the gap between each seasons from 1998 till 2017.
